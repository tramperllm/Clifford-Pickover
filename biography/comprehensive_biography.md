**Personal Information:**

- **Family:** Married Elahe Khorasani (computer programmer) on June 2, 1985

### Educational Journey

**Early Education:**
- Primary education in New Jersey public schools
- Demonstrated early aptitude for mathematics and science
- Active participation in science fairs and mathematics competitions

**Higher Education:**
- **Franklin and Marshall College** (Lancaster, Pennsylvania)
  - Bachelor of Arts, graduated summa cum laude
  - Completed four-year program in just three years
  - Major: Chemistry/Biochemistry
  - Graduated 1979 at age 22

- **Yale University** (New Haven, Connecticut)
  - Ph.D. in Molecular Biophysics and Biochemistry (1982)
  - Dissertation research on X-ray scattering and protein structure
  - Age at graduation: 25
  - Research focused on computational approaches to molecular structure
